Pet Grooming

Our expert groomers provide full grooming services in a calm, clean environment designed to keep your pet comfortable and looking their best. Whether it's a simple bath and brush or a full breed-specific styling, every session is handled with patience and care. We use premium, pet-safe products suitable for sensitive skin and all coat types.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

Everything in this Service

Bath & Blow Dry

Full shampoo and conditioning treatment using breed-appropriate, hypoallergenic products, followed by professional blow drying and brushing to a smooth, tangle-free finish.

Breed-Specific Styling & Haircut

Precision trimming and styling following breed-standard cuts or owner-requested styles, performed by groomers trained in both dog and cat coat types.

Nail Trimming & Filing

Safe trimming of overgrown nails using sharp, proper-sized clippers, followed by filing to smooth sharp edges — preventing splitting, snagging, and joint stress.

Ear Cleaning

Gentle removal of wax and debris from the outer ear canal using veterinarian-approved ear cleaner, reducing the risk of infection and detecting early signs of otitis.

Teeth Brushing

Manual brushing with pet-safe enzymatic toothpaste to reduce plaque, freshen breath, and support ongoing oral health between professional dental cleanings.

De-shedding Treatment

Specialized shampoo, conditioning, and high-velocity drying combined with thorough brushing to significantly reduce shedding and remove loose undercoat in heavy-shedding breeds.
